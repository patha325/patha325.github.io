Flights to and from Scotland

The main airports are Glasgow Intl and Edinburgh Intl, both are equally far from the Glenskirlie. Pick the flights after price, it is quite easy to travel from the airport into the city and also easy to travel between the cities.

  • The following airlines fly between Stockholm and Edinburgh or Glasgow:
  • Norwegian
  • KLM
  • SAS
  • There may be others, however the above are recommended by us.

Hotels in Glasgow

It is recommended that you stay in Glasgow or Glenskirlie around the day of the wedding as transportation will be arranged between Glasgow and Glenskirlie on the day of the wedding. More details will be given closer to the date.

Apart from the regular hotel chains, also check out the following:

  • Premier inn
  • Travel lodge
